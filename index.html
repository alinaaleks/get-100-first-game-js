Game Rules

  1. Roll the dice. If it is not "1" - it becomes your current score.
  2. Each following roll adds up to a current score.
  3. If "1" is" rolled - round is lost and turn goes to another player.
  4. Player should hold the score before the dice rolls "1".
  5. Player with a score of 100 wins.
